Dapoli

~A coastal town with pristine beaches and historical significance.

Dapoli Beach

Dapoli Beach is a serene and picturesque beach known for its golden sands and clear waters. It is a perfect spot for relaxation and water sports.
Fun Fact:
~Dapoli is often referred to as the 'Mini Mahabaleshwar' due to its lush greenery and pleasant climate! Things to Explore at Dapoli Beach:

  • Water Sports:
    Enjoy thrilling water sports like jet skiing, banana boat rides, and parasailing.
  • Dolphin Watching:
    Take a boat ride to spot playful dolphins in their natural habitat.
  • Sunset Views:
    Witness breathtaking sunsets over the Arabian Sea.
  • Local Cuisine:
    Savor delicious seafood and local delicacies at beach shacks.

Why Visit Dapoli Beach?
!Because it’s a perfect blend of adventure, relaxation, and natural beauty!

Keshavraj Temple

Keshavraj Temple is a historic temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, located amidst lush greenery. It is a popular pilgrimage site and a great spot for spiritual seekers.
Fun Fact:
~The temple is believed to be over 1000 years old and is known for its intricate carvings! Things to Explore at Keshavraj Temple:

  • Spiritual Experience:
    Experience the spiritual energy and tranquility of the temple.
  • Heritage Architecture:
    Admire the intricate carvings and architecture of the temple.
  • Nature Trails:
    Explore nearby nature trails and enjoy the serene environment.

Why Visit Keshavraj Temple?
