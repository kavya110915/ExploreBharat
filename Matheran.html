Matheran

~Asia's only automobile-free hill station, offering a peaceful retreat.

Panorama Point

Panorama Point is one of the most popular viewpoints in Matheran, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. It is a perfect spot for sunrise and sunset views.
Fun Fact:
~It is the highest point in Matheran, providing breathtaking views of the Western Ghats! Things to Explore at Panorama Point:

  • Scenic Views:
    Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.
  • Photography:
    Capture stunning photographs of the landscape.
  • Nature Trails:
    Explore nearby nature trails and enjoy the serene environment.
  • Picnic Spots:
    Relax at designated picnic spots and enjoy the tranquility.

Why Visit Panorama Point?
!Because it’s a perfect blend of natural beauty and serenity in Matheran.

Charlotte Lake

Charlotte Lake is a serene lake located in Matheran, surrounded by lush greenery and hills. It is a popular spot for picnics and nature walks.
Fun Fact:

Matheran Railway

Matheran Railway is a narrow-gauge railway that connects Matheran to Neral. It is one of the oldest railways in India and offers a scenic journey through the hills.
Fun Fact:
~The railway was built in 1907 and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site! Things to Explore at Matheran Railway:

  • Scenic Journey:
    Enjoy a scenic train ride through the hills.
  • Photography:
    Capture stunning photographs of the railway and surrounding landscape.
  • Heritage Experience:
    Experience the rich history and heritage of the railway.
  • Local Markets:
    Explore nearby markets for local handicrafts and souvenirs.

Why Visit Matheran Railway?
!Because it’s a perfect blend of history, heritage, and natural beauty in Matheran.
