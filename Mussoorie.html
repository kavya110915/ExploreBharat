Gun Hill

Gun Hill is the second highest peak in Mussoorie, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. It can be reached by a cable car or a short trek.
Fun Fact:
~Gun Hill got its name from the cannon that was fired every day at noon during the British era! Things to Explore at Gun Hill:

  • Scenic Views:
    Enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayas and the Doon Valley.
  • Cable Car Ride:
    Experience a thrilling cable car ride to the top of Gun Hill.
  • Local Shops:
    Explore local shops selling souvenirs and handicrafts.
  • Photography:
    Capture stunning photographs of the landscape and sunset views.

Why Visit Gun Hill?
!Because it’s a perfect spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts in Mussoorie!

Kempty Falls

Kempty Falls is a beautiful waterfall located near Mussoorie. It is a popular picnic spot and offers a refreshing escape from the summer heat.
Fun Fact:
~Kempty Falls was established as a picnic spot by the British in the 19th century! Things to Explore at Kempty Falls:

  • Swimming:
    Enjoy swimming in the natural pools formed by the waterfall.
  • Boating:
    Take a boat ride in the nearby lake for a serene experience.
  • Local Cuisine:
    Savor local delicacies at the food stalls near the falls.
  • Photography:
    Capture stunning photographs of the waterfall and surrounding landscape.

Why Visit Kempty Falls?
!Because it’s a perfect spot for relaxation, adventure, and enjoying nature’s beauty!

Christ Church
