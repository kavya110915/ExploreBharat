Nainital

~Scenic lake town surrounded by hills

Naini Lake

Naini Lake is a picturesque lake located in the heart of Nainital. It is surrounded by hills and offers boating facilities, making it a popular tourist attraction.
Fun Fact:
~The lake is shaped like an eye, which is why it is called Naini Lake! Things to Explore at Naini Lake:

  • Boating:
    Enjoy a serene boat ride on the lake.
  • Lakeside Walks:
    Take leisurely walks along the lakeside promenade.
  • Local Markets:
    Explore nearby markets for local handicrafts and souvenirs.
  • Photography:
    Capture stunning photographs of the lake and surrounding hills.

Why Visit Naini Lake?
!Because it’s a perfect blend of natural beauty and tranquility in Nainital.

Naina Devi Temple

Naina Devi Temple is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Naina Devi, located on the northern shore of Naini Lake. It is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas and attracts numerous pilgrims.
Fun Fact:
~The temple is believed to be the site where the eyes of Goddess Sati fell! Things to Explore at Naina Devi Temple:

  • Spiritual Atmosphere:
    Experience the spiritual energy and tranquility of the temple.
  • Scenic Views:
    Enjoy breathtaking views of Naini Lake from the temple premises.
  • Local Markets:
    Explore nearby markets for religious items and souvenirs.
  • Photography:
    Capture stunning photographs of the temple and its surroundings.

Why Visit Naina Devi Temple?
!Because it’s a perfect blend of spirituality, culture, and natural beauty in Nainital.

Snow View Point

Snow View Point is a popular viewpoint in Nainital that offers panoramic views of the snow-capped Himalayan peaks. It can be reached by a cable car or a short trek.
Fun Fact:
~On clear days, you can see peaks like Nanda Devi and Trishul from here! Things to Explore at Snow View Point:

  • Scenic Views:
    Enjoy breathtaking views of the snow-capped mountains.
  • Cable Car Ride:
    Experience an exhilarating cable car ride to the viewpoint.
  • Photography:

Why Visit Snow View Point?
!Because it’s a perfect spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts in Nainital.

Nainital Zoo

Nainital Zoo, also known as Pt. Govind Ballabh Pant Zoo, is a high-altitude zoo located in Nainital. It is home to various species of animals and birds, including the endangered snow leopard.
Fun Fact:
~It is one of the few zoos in India located at such a high altitude! Things to Explore at Nainital Zoo:

Why Visit Nainital Zoo?
