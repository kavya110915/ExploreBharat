Patiala

Qila Mubarak

Qila Mubarak is a historical fort that showcases the grandeur of Patiala’s royal heritage. It features stunning architecture, intricate carvings, and beautiful gardens.
Fun Fact:
~It was built in the 18th century and served as the residence of the Patiala royal family! Things to Explore at Qila Mubarak:

  • Fort Architecture:
    Admire the intricate carvings and frescoes that adorn the fort walls.
  • Museum:
    Explore the museum that houses artifacts and memorabilia from the royal family.
  • Gardens:
    Stroll through the beautifully landscaped gardens surrounding the fort.
  • Light and Sound Show:
    Experience the captivating light and sound show that narrates the history of Patiala.

Why Visit Qila Mubarak?
!Because it’s not just a fort; it’s a glimpse into the opulent lifestyle of the Patiala royals, making it a must-visit for history enthusiasts and tourists alike.

Sheesh Mahal

Sheesh Mahal, also known as the Glass Palace, is a stunning palace adorned with intricate glasswork and mirrors. It reflects the artistic brilliance of the Patiala royal family.
Fun Fact:
~The palace was built in the 19th century and is famous for its exquisite glass ceilings and walls! Things to Explore at Sheesh Mahal:

  • Glasswork:
    Marvel at the intricate glasswork that adorns the walls and ceilings of the palace.
  • Museum:
    Visit the museum that showcases artifacts from the Patiala royal family.
  • Gardens:
    Relax in the beautifully landscaped gardens surrounding the palace.
  • Photography:
    Capture stunning photographs of the palace’s unique architecture.

Why Visit Sheesh Mahal?
!Because it’s not just a palace; it’s a masterpiece of art and architecture that offers a glimpse into the royal heritage of Patiala, making it a must-visit for art lovers and history buffs.

Baradari Garden

Baradari Garden is a beautiful public park in Patiala, known for its lush greenery, flower beds, and serene ambiance. It’s a perfect place for families to relax and enjoy nature.
Fun Fact:
~The garden was designed in the Mughal style, reminiscent of the famous gardens in Delhi! Things to Explore at Baradari Garden:

  • Flower Beds:
    Admire the vibrant flower beds that bloom throughout the year.
  • Walking Trails:
    Enjoy leisurely walks along the well-maintained walking trails.
  • Picnic Spots:
    Relax and have a picnic in designated areas with benches and tables.
  • Children’s Play Area:
    Let your kids have fun in the dedicated play area with swings and slides.

Why Visit Baradari Garden?
!Because it’s not just a garden; it’s a peaceful retreat that offers a perfect blend of nature and recreation, making it an ideal getaway for relaxation.

Gurudwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib

Gurudwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib is a significant Sikh shrine in Patiala, known for its spiritual ambiance and historical importance. It attracts devotees and tourists alike.
Fun Fact:
~The gurudwara is built on the site where Guru Tegh Bahadur Ji, the ninth Sikh Guru, visited! Things to Explore at Gurudwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib:

  • Spiritual Atmosphere:
    Experience the serene and peaceful environment of the gurudwara.
  • Langar:
    Enjoy the community meal (langar) served to all visitors.
  • Historical Significance:
    Learn about the history and significance of the gurudwara through guided tours.
  • Architecture:
    Admire the beautiful architecture and intricate carvings of the gurudwara.

Why Visit Gurudwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib?
!Because it’s not just a religious site; it’s a place of peace and spirituality that offers a unique cultural experience, making it a must-visit for everyone.

Nehru Rose Garden

Nehru Rose Garden is a beautiful public park in Patiala, known for its vibrant rose gardens and lush greenery. It’s a perfect place for families to relax and enjoy nature.
Fun Fact:
