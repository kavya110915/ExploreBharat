These temples were not just religious centers but also hubs of cultural activity, music, and learning during the Chola reign. The use of granite in their construction, despite the absence of nearby quarries, reflects the remarkable logistics and craftsmanship of the time.

Today, the Great Living Chola Temples remain vibrant places of worship while also attracting historians, architects, and tourists from across the world who admire their unparalleled beauty and historical importance.
